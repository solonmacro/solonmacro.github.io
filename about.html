About SolonInsight

SolonInsight is an independent macroeconomic monitoring platform focused on financial stability, systemic risk, and economic cycles.

The project tracks a curated set of macroeconomic, credit, labor market, and liquidity indicators to provide a clear, data-driven view of underlying risk conditions in the global financial system.

The name Solon is inspired by the ancient Athenian lawmaker and reformer known for addressing debt imbalances and promoting long-term economic stability through measured reform. In the same spirit, SolonInsight aims to look beyond short-term market narratives and focus on structural forces shaping economic outcomes.

What SolonInsight Does

  • Monitors key indicators across credit markets, labor markets, and liquidity conditions
  • Aggregates publicly available economic data into structured signals
  • Presents summarized risk assessments updated on a regular schedule

What SolonInsight Does Not Do

SolonInsight does not provide investment advice, financial recommendations, or trading signals. All content is provided for informational and educational purposes only.

Independence and Transparency

The platform is independent and may be partially funded through advertising. Advertising relationships do not influence data selection, methodology, or analysis. Data sources and methods are documented to allow independent verification.
